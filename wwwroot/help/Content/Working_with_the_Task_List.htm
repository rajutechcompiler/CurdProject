Working with the Task List

The Task List shows system-generated tasks that are waiting for you to take action on: click on a task to display the related report.

By default, the Task List is shown, in collapsed format, at the top of every screen. Click Show to expand it to show details of the tasks, click Hide to collapse it again.

The Task List also includes an "Info" section that shows your user name, the server and database to which you are connected, the version of the product you are using, and a list of request-related tasks, color-coded to indicate urgency.
