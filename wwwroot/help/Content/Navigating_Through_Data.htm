Navigating Through Data

By default, data is displayed in a grid (rows and columns): to display data in a form, click Show Form. The grid is still visible. When Form view is open, clicking on another record in the grid displays that record's data in the form. To close the Form view, click Hide Form. The choice of layout is reset when you move to a different view.

Manipulating Columns

When viewing data in a grid, you can use a variety of controls to change the sort order or resize columns.

Changing the Sort Order

To sort the data by column, click on a column heading; click again to reverse the sort order.

Note:  Columns that can be used for sorting have a icon beside the heading. The sort column has a or beside the heading.

Resizing a Column

Click in the divider between columns and drag the mouse pointer to resize the column.
