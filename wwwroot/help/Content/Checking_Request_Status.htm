Checking Current Location, Wait List and Tracking History of Record

When viewing any table containing trackable records, below the records list is a Tracking Viewer panel. If it is not visible, click and select Show Tracking.

To expand the panel, click Show (in the top right corner of the panel).

The panel shows:

  • the current location of the record, who requested it and when it is due back (if applicable)
  • each user who is waiting for the record (click Request Details to see more information)
  • the history of who has had possession of the record

Alternatively, you can generate a report:

  • the Past Due Tracked Objects report shows records that have been checked out and are due as of a specific date (which may be in the future, although the primary purpose of the report is to look for overdue items
  • the Objects Out report, which lists all records checked out to employees and shows who checked the record out, how to contact that person, when it was checked out and other information
