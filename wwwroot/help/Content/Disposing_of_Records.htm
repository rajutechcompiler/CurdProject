Disposing of Records

Note:  TAB FusionRMS maintains an audit report to check if the disposition policy has been observed.

Generating Eligible for Final Disposition Report

This report finds all records eligible for disposition, across all database tables that have retention rules assigned.

  1. Click Reports > Retention Reports > Eligible for Final Disposition.
  2. Click New Archival Report or New Purge Report to see the records that are eligible for archive or purge.
  3. All eligible records are displayed. Select the ones that you want to dispose of. Note: see below for further retention hold options .
  4. Print the report for signature (authorize disposition) and for use to indicate which records have been removed.

When the disposition has been authorized and the physical records removed, retrieve the report and click Submit for Disposition.

Producing Evidence of Disposition

To produce a document that can demonstrate to the appropriate authorities that a record existed at one point but is no longer accessible:

  1. Click Reports > Retention Reports > Certificates of Disposition.
  2. Select the date for which you want to produce a certificate.
  3. Click Print.

Have the report signed and forwarded to the appropriate party. The report’s checkbox will indicate if an electronic record was involved.

Note:  If this report is run before disposition is complete, the certificate will indicate it represents an incomplete batch.

Preventing Disposition for Selected Records

Although it is possible to, in the Eligible for Final Disposition report, deselect a record subject to disposition, you can also place individual records on a retention hold to temporarily prevent them from being subject to disposition. Alternatively, you can “snooze” its disposition to delay it for a period of time.

  1. Open a table subject to disposition, select the record, click Show Form, then click Retention Info.
  2. Click Add.
  3. Click Retention hold or Legal hold.
  4. Enter a reason for the hold. To have the hold remain in place until manually removed, click OK. To have the hold released after a specific period of time, check Enable Snooze, set the date on which the legal hold will be released, and click Snooze.
  5. Click OK, then OK again.
