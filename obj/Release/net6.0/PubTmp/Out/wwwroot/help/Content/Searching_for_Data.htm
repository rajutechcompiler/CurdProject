Searching for Data

Note:  Depending on your system configuration, some tables and fields may not be searchable.

A search bar is provided above the navigation panel to help you quickly find records that contain the information you are looking for.

  1. Enter your search criteria. Some tips:
    • Characters such as * or ? will be searched for as literals, not as wildcards.
    • Narrow your results by using single or double quotes to search for an exact word or set of words.
    • Use - before a word to exclude all results that include that word.
    • To search for a record based on its ID, enter "id:" followed by the record ID you are looking for.
    • Multiple words are treated as “AND”; that is, only results that match all entries will be displayed.

  2. Use the checkboxes below the search field to:

    • Expand the search to include attachment names and, depending on configuration, text within attachments (attachments must have been processed through OCR to make their contents searchable)
    • If a view is open, you can constrain your search to the current table or the selected row.
  3. Press Enter or click . You will see a list of all matches for the data found in all records, showing the table in which it was found and the context for the data.

  4. Click on a hyperlink to open the view showing just that record, or click Show All to open the view and show all the found matches.
