Working with the Tracking Viewer

The panel shows:

  • the current location of the record, who requested it and when it is due back (if applicable)
  • each user who is waiting for the record

You can perform the following functions:

  • request or transfer the record (click Request or Transfer)
  • see the history of who has had possession of the record (click History)
  • see the status history of the record (click Status History)

Viewing the Contents of a Destination

To view the contents of a destination, right-click on it in the Tracking Viewer and select whether to see all contents or just specific types of contents.
