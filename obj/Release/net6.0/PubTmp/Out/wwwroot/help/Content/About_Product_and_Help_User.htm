About TAB FusionRMS

TAB FusionRMS enables organizations to greatly improve service and access to critical information by providing instant access to electronic documents and records, physical records, location information, usage, status and user requests. It offers solutions for environments utilizing physical records management, electronic records management or a combination of both.

TAB FusionRMS is a highly configurable solution: your environment may not include all features and functions described here. In addition, access to functions is controlled by user permissions, so you may not be able to perform all the functions described. Questions about functions and access should be directed to your TAB FusionRMS administrator.
