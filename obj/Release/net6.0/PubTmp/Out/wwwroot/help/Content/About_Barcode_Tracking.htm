About Barcode Tracking and Requests

Barcode tracking is used to monitor the locations of records representing physical objects and their destination (if any): both records and destinations need to have associated barcodes. To facilitate this process, each record, destination (location) and user involved in the tracking process is assigned a unique identifier. A “destination” can be a physical location or the person who has requested and has possession of the physical object: both destinations/locations and requestors need to be set up for transfers. Designated users are responsible for fulfilling these requests.

The Tracking Viewer allows you to see the current location of an object. You can also view the record of the object’s movement, and details of each action (who took it and when).
