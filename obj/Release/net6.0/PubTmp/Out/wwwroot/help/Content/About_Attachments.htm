About Attachments

Attachments are, or represent, electronic or paper documents associated with a record. Attachments are indexed when loaded into TAB FusionRMS, and their contents can be searched by a full database search. Multiple attachments can be associated with a record, and there can be multiple versions of a particular attachment, with one designated as the official record.

If a record has attachments, you will see in the record row (if there are no attachments, the icon will include a +). Hover over the icon to view a preview of the attachment, or click it to open the Attachment Viewer, which provides the following functions:

  • adding or removing attachments, versions, or pages
  • printing or emailing one or more attachments (Internet Explorer only)
  • annotating or redacting attachments (Internet Explorer only)
